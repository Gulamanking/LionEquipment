COMPANY HISTORY

Lion Equipment Co. was officially established on April 12, 2023, as an emerging provider of heavy lifting and structural support services in Philippines. While relatively new in the industry, the company is built on a solid foundation of experience and expertise—led by the son of the founder of Jamilcres Inc., a respected name with over two decades of proven success in crane rental and heavy equipment operations. Starting with just a 25-ton crane, Lion Equipment Co. has grown with a strong reputation for safety, reliability, and long-term client partnerships across the Philippines.
